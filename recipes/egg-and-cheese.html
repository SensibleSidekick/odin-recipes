Egg and Cheese Breakfast Sandwich

Description

A simple breakfast sandwich my mother used to make. Great for days where you don't have a lot of energy to cook, but still want something warm and filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1-2 slices of your preferred cheese. (1 slice per egg)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Toast your bread.
  2. While your bread is toasting heat a pan with your butter over medium heat until melted. Crack your egg or eggs into the pan. Fry until your preferred done-ness.
  3. Add a slice of cheese on top of each egg. Add a few drops of water to the pan and cover with the lid for a few seconds to melt the cheese.
  4. Once cooked and melty, place eggs on your toasted bread, make into a sandwich, and enjoy!
